# 99 — NON-NEGOTIABLES.md

# Portfolio V3

## Constitution of the Project

---

# Purpose

This document overrides every other document inside the Design Bible.

If any implementation conflicts with these principles,

this document always wins.

These are not recommendations.

These are permanent architectural laws.

Every contributor,

developer,

AI coding agent,

or future version of this portfolio

must respect them.

---

# LAW 01

## Identity Before Effects

The purpose of this portfolio is to represent Nishchal.

Not GSAP.

Not Three.js.

Not shaders.

Not fancy animations.

If an effect becomes more memorable than the person,

remove the effect.

---

# LAW 02

## Content Is Sacred

The portfolio exists to communicate.

Not decorate.

Never rewrite

About

Projects

Skills

Experience

Timeline

Contact

Resume

Certifications

Social Links

SEO

without explicit approval.

Presentation may evolve.

Content does not.

---

# LAW 03

## Terminal Is Untouchable

The terminal command system is the signature interaction.

It is permanent.

Never replace it.

Never redesign it.

Never simplify it.

Never convert it into

Buttons.

Dropdowns.

Toggles.

Floating menus.

Voice commands.

AI chat.

Nothing replaces the terminal.

---

# LAW 04

## Analytics Are Untouchable

Analytics are infrastructure.

Never remove them.

Never disable them.

Never delay them.

Never rename existing events.

Animations adapt to analytics.

Analytics never adapts to animations.

---

# LAW 05

## Performance Is A Feature

60 FPS is not a goal.

It is part of the product.

If an animation risks smoothness,

the animation loses.

Performance always wins.

---

# LAW 06

## Accessibility Always Wins

No visual effect is more important than accessibility.

If a conflict exists,

accessibility wins.

Immediately.

Without discussion.

---

# LAW 07

## Atmosphere Supports

Atmosphere never leads.

Particles.

Weather.

Lighting.

Spider.

Glass.

Fog.

Everything exists only to support content.

Never replace content.

Never delay content.

Never distract from content.

---

# LAW 08

## Motion Must Explain

Every movement answers a question.

Where?

Why?

How?

If motion cannot answer,

remove it.

Decorative motion is temporary.

Meaningful motion is timeless.

---

# LAW 09

## Reading Is Sacred

Whenever visitors read,

the interface becomes quieter.

Nothing competes with typography.

Nothing interrupts concentration.

Reading has absolute priority.

---

# LAW 10

## Scroll Belongs To The Visitor

Never hijack scrolling.

Never force long animations.

Never lock the visitor.

Scrolling belongs to the human.

The website adapts.

Not the opposite.

---

# LAW 11

## Less Is Premium

When uncertain,

remove.

Not add.

Premium products communicate through restraint.

Not abundance.

---

# LAW 12

## Every Pixel Has Purpose

Nothing exists

because

it looks cool.

Everything exists

because

it improves the experience.

If purpose disappears,

the element disappears.

---

# LAW 13

## Glass Is Material

Glass is not decoration.

Glass communicates hierarchy.

Elevation.

Depth.

Never cover the interface with glass.

Use it intentionally.

---

# LAW 14

## Spider Is Wildlife

The spider is not UI.

The spider is not branding.

The spider is not navigation.

It simply lives inside the workspace.

Respect that illusion.

Never gamify it.

---

# LAW 15

## Preserve Existing Identity

Portfolio V3 is an enhancement.

Never a replacement.

Never rebuild what already works.

Improve.

Refine.

Polish.

Preserve.

---

# LAW 16

## Every Engine Has One Responsibility

Motion.

Atmosphere.

Spider.

Glass.

Theme.

Commands.

Analytics.

Each owns one concern.

Nothing more.

---

# LAW 17

## Animation Never Delays Interaction

Buttons respond instantly.

Navigation responds instantly.

Terminal responds instantly.

Clicks respond instantly.

Interaction begins immediately.

Animation follows.

Never the opposite.

---

# LAW 18

## Technology Must Disappear

Visitors should never think

"This uses GSAP."

"This uses Lenis."

"This uses Three.js."

Technology disappears.

Craftsmanship remains.

---

# LAW 19

## Curiosity Is Rewarded

Visitors who spend time exploring

discover more.

Never announce discoveries.

Never reward discoveries.

Never explain discoveries.

Let curiosity remain personal.

---

# LAW 20

## Consistency Is Luxury

Luxury is consistency.

Not complexity.

Spacing.

Motion.

Glass.

Lighting.

Typography.

Everything should feel like it belongs to one product.

---

# LAW 21

## Failure Must Be Graceful

If

Spider fails

Weather fails

Glass fails

Particles fail

Animations fail

The portfolio remains beautiful.

Enhancements may fail.

The experience may not.

---

# LAW 22

## AI Must Respect Human Decisions

AI assists.

AI never redesigns.

AI never rewrites identity.

AI never changes the soul of the portfolio.

Creative direction belongs to the human.

Implementation belongs to the machine.

---

# LAW 23

## Future Features Must Earn Their Place

Every new feature must answer

Why should this exist?

What problem does it solve?

What emotion does it improve?

What existing rule does it respect?

If these questions cannot be answered,

the feature is rejected.

---

# LAW 24

## The Visitor Comes First

The portfolio is built

for the visitor.

Not for Lighthouse.

Not for Dribbble.

Not for Awwwards.

Not for social media.

Metrics matter.

Humans matter more.

---

# LAW 25

## The Portfolio Must Age Gracefully

The design should still feel modern

five years from now.

Avoid trends.

Avoid hype.

Avoid temporary aesthetics.

Build timeless experiences.

---

# LAW 26

## Engineering Is Part Of Design

Clean architecture

is visible

through reliability.

Maintainability

is part of craftsmanship.

Future developers should enjoy working on this project.

---

# LAW 27

## Nothing Is Allowed To Feel Random

Animations.

Spacing.

Lighting.

Color.

Timing.

Transitions.

Everything should feel intentional.

Visitors may not understand why.

They should simply feel quality.

---

# LAW 28

## Respect Silence

Not every second needs movement.

Not every corner needs decoration.

Not every interaction needs animation.

Silence creates contrast.

Contrast creates beauty.

---

# LAW 29

## The Website Is A Workspace

Not a game.

Not a movie.

Not an advertisement.

Not an animation demo.

It is a digital workspace.

Protect that identity.

Always.

---

# LAW 30

## The Final Question

Before merging any feature,

ask one question.

> "Would this make someone trust Nishchal more?"

If the answer is

No,

the feature does not belong here.

---

# THE OATH

Every future contribution to Portfolio V3 should protect the following values.

Craftsmanship over complexity.

Quality over quantity.

Purpose over decoration.

Performance over effects.

Clarity over trends.

Consistency over novelty.

Human experience over technology.

Identity over imitation.

Timelessness over hype.

---

# FINAL DECLARATION

Portfolio V3 is not an experiment.

It is not a template.

It is not a trend.

It is a long-term digital identity.

Every line of code should protect that identity.

Every animation should respect that identity.

Every future version should strengthen that identity.

Everything else is secondary.
